OUR STORY
Established in 1998, PT Inti Jasa Mandiri has become one of the leaders in providing reliable freight services to various worldwide destinations. We provide a competitive freight rate with a service none to others. Headquartered in Surabaya, East Java - Indonesia's second-biggest city, PT Inti Jasa Mandiri's excellence has been acknowledged by various companies. Our company's objective is to become a partner that can be relied on for all your shipment needs.
